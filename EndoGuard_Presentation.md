# EndoGuard: The Internal Application Shield
**Project Presentation**

---

## Slide 1: Front Page
### **EndoGuard**
#### *Next-Generation Embedded Security Framework*

**Purpose:** Protecting applications from the inside out.
**Identity:** Open-source, Low-tech, High-impact.

---

## Slide 2: The Core Problem
### **The Perimeter is Failing**
*   **The Moat is Dry:** Firewalls and WAFs can't see "Inside" the application logic.
*   **The Shadow Threat:** Credential stuffing, account takeovers, and business logic fraud use "legitimate" looking traffic.
*   **The Blind Spot:** Traditional tools lack the context of User identity and behavioral history.

---

## Slide 3: The Solution
### **Introducing EndoGuard**
*   **Embedded Security:** Lives inside your product, not just in front of it.
*   **Behavioral Identity:** Focuses on *what* users do, not just *who* they say they are.
*   **Real-time Decisioning:** Evaluates risk at the moment an event occurs.

---

## Slide 4: Tech Stack
### **Reliable & Scalable Architecture**
*   **Core:** PHP 8.1+ / PostgreSQL 12+
*   **Logic:** Fat-Free Framework (F3) - Light & Fast.
*   **Security:** Ruler Engine (Flexible business rules).
*   **Intelligence:** Matomo Device Detector (Precise fingerprints).
*   **Agility:** Full Docker & Heroku support.

---

## Slide 5: The Workflow
### **The Journey of an Event**
1.  **Sense:** SDK triggers on a critical action.
2.  **Enrich:** IP, Geolocation, and Device context added.
3.  **Evaluate:** Rule engine calculates risk score.
4.  **Act:** Automated block or manual review flag.
5.  **Visualize:** Live update on the Admin Dashboard.
